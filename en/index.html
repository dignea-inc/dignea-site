The crisis, right now

740 residences closed, nearly one a week

More than 1,400 senior tenants evicted in 2024. About 1,350 residences remain to meet needs that are exploding. Dignéa is designed to help those that remain fill their rooms, and families find them.

The real issue

The problem isn't supply. It's the connection.

Families, residences and public-network professionals operate in silos. Information flows poorly, decisions are made under pressure, and intermediaries fill the gap by monetizing the confusion. Dignéa is the opposite: a single layer, designed to connect everyone without commission. Whatever door you come through, you reach the right place.
